About Melody Chocolate

Melody Chocolate is a delightful confectionery made from the finest cocoa beans sourced from around the world. Our chocolate is crafted with passion and expertise, ensuring a rich and indulgent taste experience for chocolate lovers of all ages.

Our journey began over a century ago when our founder, John Melody, embarked on a quest to create the perfect chocolate recipe. Today, Melody Chocolate stands as a symbol of quality and excellence, beloved by millions worldwide.

Nutritional Information

Indulging in Melody Chocolate not only satisfies your sweet cravings but also provides essential nutrients to support your well-being. Here are the average nutritional values per serving (100g):

  • Calories: 520
  • Total Fat: 30g
  • Saturated Fat: 18g
  • Cholesterol: 15mg
  • Sodium: 30mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 60g
  • Sugars: 50g
  • Protein: 5g

Enjoy Melody Chocolate as part of a balanced diet and savor the goodness in every bite!
